About

Product thinking, grounded in operations

I'm an Associate Product Manager who builds AI-powered SaaS products for Co-Living and Hospitality Technology. My work sits where residents, staff, and operations meet — housekeeping, maintenance, audits, food, and laundry — and my job is to turn those workflows into products that are measurably better to run.

I own the full product lifecycle: leading product discovery and user research, gathering business requirements, writing PRDs, BRDs, and user stories, and translating them into roadmaps the team can ship. I run Agile and Scrum with engineering and design, define KPIs, and make data-driven decisions backed by SQL and product analytics.

With a background in Mathematics and Computer Science, I bridge business strategy and technical execution — prioritising features, managing stakeholders, and delivering AI products that increase user adoption, improve operational efficiency, and reduce costs.
